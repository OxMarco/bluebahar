{"pages":1,"text":"PORTS AND YACHTING DIRECTORATE\nLOCAL NOTICE TO MARINERS No 097 of 2025\nOur Ref: TM/PYD/ 0001/25\n23 June 2025\nFurther to Local Notice to Mariners No 095 of 2021 - Illicit Discharges\nThe Ports and Yachting Directorate, Transport Malta would like to remind all mariners\nand operators of recreational craft that it is prohibited to dispose any type of waste into\nthe marine environment.\nAll waste shall be disposed ashore in the appropriate waste reception facilities.\nFurthermore, all concerned are being advised that the discharge of treated and untreated\nsewage within bays, harbours and ports, including yachting centres, and on anchor is\nalso prohibited. Discharge of sewage is only allowed as follows:\ni. The discharge of untreated sewage from recreational craft is only allowed in waters\nmore than 1NM from the shore, but not whilst on anchor.\nii. Vessels fitted with an onboard sewage treatment plant are allowed to discharge\nsewage anywhere outside of bays, harbours and ports, but not whilst on anchor.\nEnforcement and legal action in terms of law will be taken if any person is caught\ndisposing of any type of waste in the marine environment or if there is sufficient evidence\nof any illegal disposal at sea.\nAll the other information mentioned in Local Notice to Mariners No 095 of 2021 will remain\nvalid.\n\n-- 1 of 1 --"}
