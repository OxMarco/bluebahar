mariners shall, in addition\nto the above Directives, comply with the following accordingly: -\n- Fully comply with the Requirements of the Collision Regulations for the Prevention\nof Collisions at Sea COLREGS 72 (as amended) concerning Lights, Shapes and\nSound Signals.\n- The Rules for Lights shall be complied with from sunset to sunrise, and during such\ntimes no other lights shall be exhibited, except such lights as cannot be mistaken\nfor the lights specified in the Rules or do not impair their visibility or distinctive\ncharacter or interfere with the keeping of a proper look-out.\n- The use of ultra-long throw searchlights, laser, strobe, flashing and bright colored\nlights is prohibited in the areas as indicated on the attached charts.\n- Loud music, horns, sirens, and bells, other than as required by the Rules, are also\nprohibited in the areas as indicted on the charts.\n- Observation of any speed restrictions.\n- Maintaining a distance off as prescribed in this Notice.\n- Action will be taken against anybody not observing these restrictions. Offenders\nmay be liable to legal prosecution.\n\n-- 1 of 13 --\n\nBlue Grotto – Qrendi - Chart 1\nThe restricted area is shown in red.
